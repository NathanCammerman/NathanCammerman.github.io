Personal development

In the Hogent we have a subject called ITalent. This course is about self-development, cultural differences, theoretically lauching an application, start-ups, how different people use the internet, etc. Below are the various phases that I would like to discuss and which reflect my evaluation.

Phase 1: Individual and IT

There is a professional personality test made with 4 different results. Dominant, Influential, Stable or Conscientious. I scored about the same for each trait. However, my Conscientious trait stood out.





Reflection Phase 1

This means that I am a perfectionist. It's true because I want to know every detail so that my work can be as perfect as possible. We have also learned to work with people with a different quality than our own. This was of course no problem for me because of all my experience.

Phase 2.1: International and IT

We had to carry out a study on the power difference between 2 countries. I chose France and Denmark.

If you want to see the result of my research you can always click here for the PDF document.






Phase 2.2: Cultural differences

In this assignment the incentive was to explain two intercultural conflicts that we experienced.

For my experience in and reflection on this situation you can always click here for the PDF document.

Phase 3: Business and IT & InnovIT

Throughout the second semester we had to come up with an application in a group and then work it out the business side. Our application is called "deten". For more info click here. We were expected to think in group, to advertise, to pitch, ...

My contribution

In addition to all mandatory assignments, I also pitched our application to fellow students, teachers and a jury, which makes a total of 75 people.

Enneagram

This was a test to observe our evolution as a student. I have scored 19 points on perfectionist, 19 on questioner and 3 on romantic. Questioner and perfectionist are certainly correct. I always want to complete my work to perfection and I think asking questions is the best way to gain knowledge!

Competence indicator

This test showed that I am primarily a team player. This also corresponds to how I feel about it. I learn much more and better in a team than alone. This of course does not mean that I cannot take my responsibility alone!

MBTI

I achieved the ENTP result. This means Extrovert, Intuitive, Thinking and Perception. These characteristics correspond well with how I have evolved over the years. I have become more loose and act more logically rather than on feeling. I am intuitive and broad-minded!

Ofman

We had to make the Ofman test to make us think about all our pros and cons. What you are good at, what you blame yourself for, ... This provided a very insightful experience.
